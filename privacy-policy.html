Privacy Policy for Decision Dating App

Last updated: April 21, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use our Decision Dating application ("App").

Information We Collect

Our App collects the following information:

  • Audio Recordings: When you press the record button, we temporarily collect the audio of your speech for the purpose of transcription.
  • Device Permissions: We request microphone access to enable audio recording functionality.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Transcription Services: Your audio recordings are processed locally on iOS devices using the device's built-in speech recognition when available. In cases where native speech recognition is unavailable or fails, audio may be sent to OpenAI's servers for transcription processing via their Whisper API.
  • Providing App Functionality: To convert your speech to text within the App.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We share your information as follows:

  • OpenAI: When native speech recognition is unavailable or fails, your audio recordings may be sent to OpenAI for processing through their Whisper API. OpenAI's use of this data is governed by their privacy policy.
  • Apple: When using iOS devices, your speech may be processed using Apple's on-device speech recognition technology. This processing happens locally on your device and audio data is not sent to Apple's servers unless you have enabled "Improve Siri & Dictation" in your iOS settings.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any other third parties.

Data Retention

  • Audio Recordings: Audio recordings are not stored on our servers. They are temporarily processed and then discarded immediately after transcription.
  • Transcribed Text: Transcribed text remains solely on your device and is not stored on our servers.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access information we have about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to object to processing

Third-Party Services

Our App uses OpenAI's Whisper API for speech-to-text processing. Their privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy, which we encourage you to review.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
